About console screen and why is it use and how to open the console screen

To open the console in Chrome, use this keyboard shortcut: Cmd + Option + J (on a Mac) or Cmd +Shift +J (on Windows). As an alternative, you could right-click on the webpage and click "Inspect," and the developers window will appear Click the "Console" tab in that window appear. You should use the console.
